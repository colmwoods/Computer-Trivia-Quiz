Computer Trivia Quiz - Rules & Information

Welcome to the Computer Trivia Quiz! Test your knowledge about computers with this fun quiz. Here are the rules and information you need to know before you start:

How to Play:

  • Select a difficulty level: Easy, Medium, Hard, or Mixed.
  • Choose the number of questions you want to answer (Any Number From 5 To 25).
  • Click the "Start Quiz" button to begin.
  • Read each question carefully and select the answer you think is correct.
  • After answering all questions, you will be taken to a page that will tell you your score and saying how well you did.

Scoring:

  • You will receive 1 point for each correct answer in the score tab.
  • No points will be added to score but will be added to incorrect score, if user fails to answer the question correctly
  • Once you finish the quiz, your total score will be displayed at the end of the quiz.

Additional Information:

  • The quiz consists of multiple-choice questions related to computer knowledge and technology.
  • You can retake the quiz as many times as you like to improve your score.
  • Have fun and challenge yourself!
